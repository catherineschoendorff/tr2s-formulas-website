Why 2019 → 2024?

This five-year window captures a CEO's realistic transformation horizon.

2019: Pre-COVID baseline, before the EU Green Deal

Five years is sufficient for a CEO to demonstrate strategic impact on asset configuration and capital allocation.

Understanding the Metrics

TR²S and TR³S extend traditional shareholder return measurement.

TR²S and TR³S are financial metrics - not ESG scores, not sustainability ratings. They quantify how asset preservation and ecosystem restoration translate into adjusted shareholder returns.
