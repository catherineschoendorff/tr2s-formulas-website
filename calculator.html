═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION A: STOCK PERFORMANCE (TRS Calculation)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Provide exact values:
- P₀: Opening share price on first trading day of [YEAR]
- P₁: Closing share price on last trading day of [YEAR]
- D: Total dividends paid per share during [YEAR]
- Currency: [EUR/CHF/USD/GBP/DKK]
- Calculate: TRS = (P₁ - P₀ + D) / P₀

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION B: THE GOVERNANCE GATE (Capital Eligibility)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

B1. Gate 1: ROIC vs WACC
- ROIC (Return on Invested Capital) in %
- WACC (Weighted Average Cost of Capital) in %
- Spread in percentage points (ROIC minus WACC)
- Gate Status: PASS if ROIC ≥ WACC

B2. Gate 2: Free Cash Flow
- Free Cash Flow in millions (specify currency)
- Gate Status: PASS if FCF > 0

B3. Gate 3: Balance Sheet
- Net Debt / EBITDA ratio
- Gate Status: PASS if ratio < 3.0x

B4. Overall CES
- Capital Eligibility Status: 1 if all gates PASS, 0 if any gate FAILS

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION C: ASSET VIABILITY FACTOR (for TR²S)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Provide data to estimate each factor on a 0.00 to 1.00 scale:

C1. Asset Mix Shift (RAR - Regenerative Asset Ratio)
- What percentage of assets are circular/regenerative vs extractive/linear?
- Examples: recycled input materials, renewable energy assets, remanufacturing facilities
- Provide evidence and estimate RAR (0.00 to 1.00)

C2. Useful Life Extension (LVE - Lifetime Value Extension)
- Does the company extend product/asset lifetimes beyond industry baseline?
- Examples: durability design, repair services, refurbishment programs, modularity
- Provide evidence and estimate LVE (0.00 to 1.00)

C3. Future Revenue Share (CRR - Circular Revenue Ratio)
- What percentage of revenue comes from circular business models?
- Examples: leasing, subscription, take-back, recycling services, secondary materials sales
- Provide evidence and estimate CRR (0.00 to 1.00)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION D: SYSTEM CONDITIONS FACTOR (for TR³S)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

D1. Business Model Exposure (PA - Purpose Alignment)
- Does the core business model profit from extraction or preservation?
- Scale: -1.0 (profits from depletion) to +1.0 (profits from regeneration)
- Provide reasoning and estimate PA

D2. Supply Chain Resilience (VCI - Value Chain Impact)
- How dependent is the company on extractive/volatile inputs?
- Scale: 0.00 (fully extractive-dependent) to 1.00 (fully circular/resilient)
- Provide evidence and estimate VCI

D3. External Risk Mitigation (ECR - Ecosystem Condition Risk)
- What is the company's exposure to regulatory, physical, and transition risks?
- Scale: 0.00 (high exposure, no mitigation) to 1.00 (low exposure or strong mitigation)
- Provide evidence and estimate ECR
