Perplexity Deep Research Prompt

Copy this prompt into Perplexity (Deep Research mode). Replace [COMPANY NAME] and [YEAR] with your target. 

Analyze [COMPANY NAME] for the fiscal year [YEAR] to calculate Capital Eligibility Status (CES) and Total Return to Shareholders (TRS).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION A: REQUIRED DATA (calculator cannot work without these)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

A1. Stock Performance Data
- P₀: Opening share price on first trading day of [YEAR] (exact date + price in local currency)
- P₁: Closing share price on last trading day of [YEAR] (exact date + price in local currency)  
- D: Total dividends paid per share during [YEAR]
- Calculate: TRS = (P₁ - P₀ + D) / P₀ × 100%

A2. Gate 1 – ROIC ≥ WACC
- ROIC (Return on Invested Capital) for [YEAR] in %
- WACC (Weighted Average Cost of Capital) estimate in %
- Spread: ROIC minus WACC in basis points
- Gate 1 Status: PASS if ROIC ≥ WACC, FAIL otherwise

A3. Gate 2 – Free Cash Flow
- Free Cash Flow (FCF) for [YEAR] in millions (EUR/USD/local currency)
- Year-over-year FCF growth in %
- Gate 2 Status: PASS if FCF > 0, FAIL otherwise

A4. Gate 3 – Leverage Ratio
- Net Debt / EBITDA ratio for [YEAR]
- Gate 3 Status: PASS if ratio < 3.0x, FAIL otherwise

A5. Capital Eligibility Status (CES)
- CES = 1 if ALL three gates PASS
- CES = 0 if ANY gate FAILS

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION B: OPTIONAL DATA (for TR²S/TR³S calibration)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

B1. For TR²S – Internal Regeneration (if data available)
- RAR: Regenerative/circular assets as % of total assets
- LVE: Product lifetime extension initiatives
- CRR: Circular revenue as % of total revenue

B2. For TR³S – External Restoration (if data available)
- Ecosystem restoration investments or commitments
- Value chain sustainability impact metrics
